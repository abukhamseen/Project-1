History of Troia. Café Troia was established in 1986 by the Troia family, natives of Naples, Italy. The family has generations-old traditions in good cooking and eating. With meticulous attention to high quality ingredients, traditional and innovative Italian cooking and unsurpassed service, Café Troia has become practically a landmark in Towson.
Quality Of Food. You are what you eat. When you dine with us, rest assured that you are eating foods that meet a higher standard than the USDA - ours. Our natural veal is fed a natural diet with no artificial hormones or antibiotics. It is humanely raised by family farmers and is carefully traced from the farm to the table..
Catering. Whether it is a small or large event, we can handle each with the panache of a small establishment and the expertise of a catering company. We believe that the cuisine we serve is as important as the setting for your event. You will enjoy the elegance of our dining room and our high quality cuisine
