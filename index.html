History of Troia.
   
    Cafe Troia was established in 1986 by the Troia family, natives of Naples, Italy. The family has generations-old traditions in good cooking and eating. With meticulous attention to high quality ingredients, traditional and innovative Italian cooking and unsurpassed service, Cafe© Troia has become practically a landmark in Towson.
